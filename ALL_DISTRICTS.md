## District Admin Credentials

After running `node scripts/seedGujaratDistricts.js`, each district will have:

**Username Format:** `{district_name}_admin`
**Password Format:** `{district_name}123`

Examples:
- Ahmedabad: `ahmedabad_admin` / `ahmedabad123`
- Rajkot: `rajkot_admin` / `rajkot123`
- Surat: `surat_admin` / `surat123`

**Note:** Districts with spaces in names use underscores:
- Chhota Udaipur: `chhota_udaipur_admin` / `chhotaudaipur123`
- Devbhoomi Dwarka: `devbhoomi_dwarka_admin` / `devbhoomidwarka123`
- Gir Somnath: `gir_somnath_admin` / `girsomnath123`

## Member ID Format

Members from each district get unique IDs:

**Format:** `24{DistrictCode}{MemberNumber}`

Examples:
- First member of Ahmedabad: `2401000001`
- First member of Rajkot: `2427000001`
- First member of Surat: `2429000001`

## Volunteer ID Format

Volunteers from each district get unique IDs:

**Format:** `24{DistrictCode}V{VolunteerNumber}`

Examples:
- First volunteer of Ahmedabad: `2401V0001`
- First volunteer of Rajkot: `2427V0001`
- First volunteer of Surat: `2429V0001`
